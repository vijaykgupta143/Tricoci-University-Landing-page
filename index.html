ABOUT TRICOCI UNIVERSITY

Tricoci University provides the most current, technically precise and versatile education available to help you on your way to becoming a licensed cosmetologist.  Upon completion of our courses located in Illinois, Indiana, and Wisconsin, including those in and near Chicago, students can have a secure foundation in all aspects of cosmetology.

TRICOCI UNIVERSITY OF BEAUTY CULTURE IS ACCREDITED BY NACCAS

Tricoci University of Beauty Culture’s Cosmetology Program will prepare you for a wide variety of careers, which may include:

  • Barber
  • Barber Apprentice
  • Barbershop Manager
  • Barbershop Owner
  • Master Barber
  • Men’s Stylist

ADMISSIONS AND FINANCIAL AID

  • Applicants must be at least 16 years of age by the first day of class (for teacher programs, must be 18).
  • Applicants must hold a high school diploma, high school transcripts of completion, or a GED certificate.
  • For teacher programs: applicants must have a current icense as a cosmetologist, esthetician, or nail technician..
